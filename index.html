A city with a 1500 sq.km area would indeed harbor a lot of households, at least in the near future, which would lead to high energy consumption, mainly electricity. For this, wind power and solar power plants are set up. Along with this, it needs to be properly distributed to every household efficiently, for which efficient pipelines would be required, which this business case aims to solve.

Such a huge city, with an area greater than the city of Delhi, India (1484 sq.km), would have people commuting to different corners via roads and would require efficient roads well connected within the city and also connecting the neighboring areas. This business case aims to reduce redundancy of paths and provide adequate, good-quality transport. It also aims to isolate the highways so that they would not interfere with the busy city.
