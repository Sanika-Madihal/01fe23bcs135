Why is this important?

2. Import and Export of Goods üåç

Since the city Chitranagari is known for its cultural heritage and handicrafts, most of the people's livelihood depends on the import of raw materials and export of the goods. The main challenges are faced due to lack of networking and limited access to market data.

Why is this important?

  • A robust import-export system ensures sustainability for the city‚Äôs economic backbone: its people.
  • Access to global markets will boost incomes and provide greater financial security.

We need a modern technology-driven import-export system that has the capacity to support Chitranagari's people and make sure that they reach the global markets. This includes providing real-time market data, improving logistics, and establishing networks to strengthen the local economy.

3. Improving Living Standards for Farmers üåæ

The farmers of Chitranagari face significant challenges in both livelihood and living conditions. Along with irregular rainfall and limited market access, many farmers live in inadequate housing with poor access to basic needs like clean water, electricity, etc.

Why is this important?

  • Farmers form the backbone of Chitranagari's economy, and their prosperity is crucial for the city's growth.
  • Improving living conditions will lead to better productivity and sustainability in agriculture.

A complete solution is needed to improve both the living conditions and the adoption of the latest farming techniques, ensuring better livelihoods while preserving economic sustainability. By focusing on infrastructural improvements and training programs, Chitranagari can secure its future as a prosperous city.
